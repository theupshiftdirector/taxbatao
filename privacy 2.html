Tax Batao ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website taxbatao.com. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our website.

Information We Collect

Our income tax calculator works entirely in your browser. The details you enter (income, deductions, salary components) are processed locally on your device and are never sent to or stored on our servers. We do not collect any personal or financial information through the calculator.

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to monitor activity on our website and improve user experience. These include:

  • Essential cookies required for the website to function
  • Analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with the website

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdSense - to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Data Security

Since all calculations are performed locally in your browser and never transmitted to our servers, your financial information remains on your device. We do not have access to any of the data you enter into the tax calculator.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
