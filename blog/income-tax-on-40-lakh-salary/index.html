New regime saves you ₹1,79,400 on a ₹40 lakh salary.

How to Save More Tax

  • Section 80C: Invest up to ₹1.5L in PPF, ELSS, NPS
  • Section 80D: Health insurance deduction up to ₹25,000
  • HRA Exemption: Claim if you live in rented house
  • Home Loan: Up to ₹2L deduction on interest (Sec 24)
  • NPS: Additional ₹50,000 under Section 80CCD(1B)

Which Regime Should You Choose?

If your total deductions exceed ₹5,98,000, old regime may be better. Otherwise, new regime with lower rates and ₹75,000 standard deduction is simpler.
