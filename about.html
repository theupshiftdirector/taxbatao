About Tax Batao

Tax Batao is a free income tax calculator built to help Indian taxpayers compare the Old and New tax regimes for FY 2025-26. Whether you are a salaried employee or a freelancer, Tax Batao gives you a clear, side-by-side breakdown of your tax liability under both regimes so you can choose the one that saves you more money.

The calculator supports HRA exemption, Section 80C, 80D, and other popular deductions under the Old Regime, as well as the updated New Regime slabs with the enhanced standard deduction. All calculations happen instantly in your browser with zero data collection.
